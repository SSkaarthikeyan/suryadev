Established in 2006, Suryadev Alloys and Power is a leading Tmt Bar Manufacturer steel manufacturer in South India, producing high-quality intermediate and finished steel products. Sponge Iron

Sponge Iron, also known as Direct Reduced Iron (DRI), is an essential raw material for manufacturing premium-grade steel. Produced by reducing high-quality iron ore without melting, it serves as a superior alternative to scrap metal in steelmaking, ensuring higher purity and consistency.

Mild Steel Billets

MS Billets are the foundation of high-quality steel products, serving as the essential raw material for manufacturing TMT bars, structural steel, and other long products. Suryadev's MS Billets are manufactured using Sponge Iron in its state-of-the-art New Gummidipoondi Steel Plant and adhere to BIS 14650 standards.

TMT Bars

TMT (Thermo-Mechanically Treated) Rebars are high-strength reinforcement bars widely used in construction due to their superior strength, flexibility, and resistance to corrosion. Suryadev TMT Rebars are available in a wide range of grades and sizes, from 8mm to 40mm in diameter, and can be customized to specific lengths to meet the unique requirements of any project.

Mild Steel Rounds

Mild Steel Rounds are versatile steel products widely used across industries for their ease of use and adaptability. Their combination of strength and flexibility makes them suitable for applications ranging from construction reinforcements to machinery components. Suryadev's MS Rounds are manufactured with precision to deliver superior quality, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial and structural applications.
